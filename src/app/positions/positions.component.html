PhD/Postdoctoral position

About The Role:

The selected candidate will join the vibrant research community of this project and will take the main responsibility of developing an integrated software tool for situational awareness in multi-agent systems, with applications in autonomous vehicles and air traffic management systems. The candidate will also work on theoretical aspects of the research that brings together logical requirements, models, and task planning algorithms in multi-agent systems. There are extensive collaboration opportunities with the researchers at the Max Planck Institute. Having previous experience of software development and excellent communication skills are expected.

Postdoctoral Position in Human-Robot Interaction

About The Role:

At the Uppsala Social Robotics Lab, we have an open postdoc position in human-robot interaction. The postdoc position is part of the Horizon Europe SymAware (Symbolic logic framework for situational awareness in mixed autonomy) project, and will be focused on human-in-the-loop design and automation methods for ethical and trustworthy human-robot interaction. Specific topics of interest include robot transparency, multimodal interaction, algorithmic fairness and gender-sensitive design to counteract gender biases in social robots.

Location:

Uppsala Social Robotics Lab Department of Information Technology Uppsala University, Sweden
