Project Consortium

Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS) studies the principles of efficient, dependable, secure, and usable computing systems, as well as their interaction with the physical and social context in which they operate. The Institute conducts foundational research in relevant areas of computer science and beyond, covering theory, empirical analysis, and data-driven investigation.

Team Leader: Dr. Sadegh Soudjani

Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan(KTH) is Sweden's largest technical research institution and has grown to become one of Europe's leading technical and engineering universities. KTH holds expertise in the areas of multi-agent planning and control with an emphasis on human-in-the-loop and formal languages based methods. KTH will be involved in the project through the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, which has been participating in many EU and national projects.

Team Leader: Prof. Dimos V. Dimarogonas

Technische Universiteit Eindhoven (TU/e) is a research-driven university of international standing, where excellent research and excellent education go hand in hand. TU/e focus on a balanced approach towards education, research and valorization of knowledge in the areas of engineering science and technology. It works in close collaboration with other universities, scientific institutes, social organizations, government, and industry.

Team Leader: Dr. Sofie Haesaert

Uppsala University (UU) is a broad research university with a clear mission: To pursue top-quality research and education and to interact constructively with society, to contribute in different ways to a better world. Uppsala University strives to bring new perspectives to the basic questions of science as well as knowledge contributing to sustainable development and human health.

Team Leader: Prof. Ginevra Castellano

Netherlands Aerospace Centre’s (NLR) mission is to make air operations and space exploration safer, more sustainable and more efficient. The multidisciplinary approach of NLR focuses on developing new and cost-effective technologies for aviation and space, from design support to production technology and MRO (Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul). With its unique expertise and state-of-the-art facilities, NLR is bridging the gap between research and application.

Team Leader: Dr. Sybert Stroeve

Siemens Industry Software Netherlands B.V. (SISW) is driving transformation to enable a digital enterprise where engineering, manufacturing and electronics design meet tomorrow. The solutions provided by SISW help companies of all sizes to create and leverage digital twins that provide organizations with new insights, opportunities, and levels of automation to drive innovation.

Team Leader: Dr. Mohsen Alirezaei
